Manarola Cinque,Terre

Northern Italy

Manarola is one of the five charming villages nestled along the rugged coastline of the Cinque Terre in Northern Italy. With its colorful houses perched on steep cliffs overlooking the Ligurian Sea, Manarola enchants visitors with its picturesque beauty and relaxed atmosphere.
