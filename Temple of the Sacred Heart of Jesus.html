Temple of the Sacred Heart of Jesus

Spain

The Temple of the Sacred Heart of Jesus, located in Barcelona, Spain, stands as a magnificent testament to Catalan modernist architecture. Designed by architect Enric Sagnier, construction of this awe-inspiring church commenced in 1902 and was completed in 1961. Perched atop the Tibidabo Mountain, the temple offers breathtaking panoramic views of Barcelona and the Mediterranean Sea.
